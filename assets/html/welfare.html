Welfare at Butler

Our Services

Butler Welfare Committee is responsible for running drop-ins, organizing campaign weeks, and distributing sexual health and sanitary supplies. You can contact the Butler Welfare team via email (butler.welfarejcr@durham.ac.uk), or via their Instagram account (@butler_welfare), where you can also find their drop-in schedules, and campaign week information.

Additional Resources

Whilst we want to help, we can not do everything. Here are a few contacts:


Do you need support right now?

For Emergency Services, call 999
If you need medical help or advice fast, call 111
Your GP (during office hours) Find a GP - NHS

If you feel you are at risk due to your mental health, or you need urgent emotional support, call:
(i) NHS 111 (Option 2)
(ii) Samaritans: 116 123 (freephone)
(iii) For text/online support: Mikeysline, Papyrus UK Suicide Prevention, and Shout
(iv) Durham Nightline: 9pm-7am, Monday to Wednesday and Saturday to Sunday during term time.

Finance

  • College student support may be able to help with emergency finance.

University Support

  • Contact jb.studentsupport@durham.ac.uk for college-based student support - they can help with university-based items especially, such as disability support, SACs, and Self-Certification of Absence.
