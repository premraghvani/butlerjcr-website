Our Facilities

Butler is proud to offer modern, accessible, and inclusive facilities to support both academic life and college community events.

The Bar

Operated by the college, and being the largest college bar in Durham. Open termtime, from 7pm to 11pm (Su-Th) or 12am (Fr, Sa), with food available until 9:30pm, with the availability to come and use the area as a study space, or a meetup place, before the main bar opens. We have two darts boards, three pool tables, and plenty of places to sit!

The JCR / MCR Lounge

The JCR (for undergraduates), and MCR (for postgraduates and mature students) each have separate lounges, kitted with sofas, board games, a TV with video games, and much more!

Library Lounge

A chill place which also has books.

Dance Studio

Gym

Butler Lawn

Right outside the college bar - a place to sit and chill - amazing in the summer!

Howlands Hall
