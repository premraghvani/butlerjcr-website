Welcome, Freshers !

We're so excited to welcome you to Josephine Butler College and the Butler JCR family.

What to Expect

Freshers' Week is packed with events, introductions, and time to get settled. You'll meet the JCR exec, college staff, and fellow freshers. From BBQs to quizzes, and club nights to chill afternoons — there's something for everyone!

What Should I Bring?

Here’s a quick checklist to help you settle in:

  • Bedding & kitchenware - you may be given the option for this
  • Important documents. You will need your passport for registration (or UK Driving / Provisional Licence for domestic students), though a passport may be handy if you are finding jobs
  • Any medication and prescriptions
  • Fancy dress items - there'll be themes!

Freshers' Events

You'll receive a full schedule on arrival, but highlights include:

  • Welcome Talks and Tours
  • JCR Fair — join clubs and societies
  • Butler Night Out
  • Chill night with board games, tea & toast

Throughout the year, many events are out there, some may be free, some may be subsidised for JCR members. If finance is an issue, do not worry as the JCR operates an access fund, and the college also has its own financial support.

Need Help?

Our welfare team and Freshers’ Reps (FREPs) are here for you. Feel free to message us on @butlerjcr or pop by the JCR office.

For any serious concerns or questions before you arrive, you can email: butler.jcr@durham.ac.uk
