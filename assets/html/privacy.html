Privacy Policy

For those of you concerned about what we are processing.

To The General Public

Defining the "General Public" as anyone but members of Durham University and Alumni.

We don't process any of your data. We don't use cookies on this public website.

Members of Durham University & Alumni

Our intranet contains all information. We use cookies on the intranet, but only an essential cookie to keep track of your session once you have logged in, retained for up to 28 days after signing in. Signing in is restricted to anyone who is a registered alumni of Josephine Butler College, or a current member of Durham University with a CIS account.

We may collect names (for those who are or have been JCR members, and/or those in college families), membership status, college family data (applicable for those being a college parent, and all college children, for children who have matriculated in October 2025 onwards), awards, and what JCR societies / sports / committees you may be part of. Please email us at the contact page if you would like to exercise any rights under the Data Protection Act 2018.
